GiotsasTech Copyright Policy

  1. Introduction

    GiotsasTech is committed to respecting and protecting the intellectual property rights of others while safeguarding our own creations. This policy outlines our approach to copyright and intellectual property management.

  2. Ownership of Copyright

    All original works created by GiotsasTech employees, contractors, or partners during their engagement with the company are the property of GiotsasTech. This includes, but is not limited to, software, documentation, designs, and multimedia content.

  3. Use of Third-Party Materials

    GiotsasTech respects the copyrights of others. Any use of third-party materials will be done in compliance with applicable copyright laws and licensing agreements. Proper attribution will be given where required.

  4. Copyright Infringement

    GiotsasTech does not tolerate copyright infringement. Any unauthorized use of copyrighted materials will be addressed promptly. Employees and contractors are required to report any suspected infringement to the legal department.

  5. Licensing and Permissions

    GiotsasTech may grant licenses for the use of its copyrighted materials under specific conditions. Requests for permission to use GiotsasTech’s copyrighted materials should be directed to the legal department.

  6. Fair Use

    GiotsasTech recognizes the fair use doctrine, which allows limited use of copyrighted materials without permission for purposes such as criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, or research. All fair use determinations will be made in accordance with applicable laws.

  7. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) Compliance

    GiotsasTech complies with the DMCA and will respond to notices of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the DMCA’s requirements. Notices should be sent to our designated DMCA agent.

  8. Policy Updates

    This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in laws or company practices. Employees and contractors will be notified of any significant changes.

  9. Contact Information

    For questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at admin@giotsastech.eu.
